My name is Nie Yushuang.I graduated from Shanghai Jiaotong university in 2014, and there I got my bachelor degree of Industrail Design. Industrail Design is a joint study area of art, engineering and leading technologies,including ergonomics,statistics,programming and physics. So I took an active part in the related elective courses and practical programmes, where I’ve acquried knowledge of multi-disciplinary, and increased my ability of fast-learning and team-work.

After graduation, I started my job career at R&D department of VSPN,an E-game entertainment and Internet company in Shanghai.During my recent two years,I progressed through e-game competition operation and related program development.There I gained a nice experience of project manamgement and improved the viewing experience with data mining and Interactive Technology. As the developement of Live Streaming and data mining technology,I feel that projects typically and increasingly in today’s society involve multiple perspectives, that is they are interdisciplinary. So I am keep learning and following up with the new leading technologies, to open up my horizon of IT technology.

Comparing with other new technologies including High-speed train,subway,cloning and optical fiber , IT has not only changed a particular field,but also linked to many other subject areas. The mix of internet technology and other research field could make a new series creation, for example, MATLAB provides a new way for the dynamic simulation of automobile for engineers, and desingers can make a quick concept model by CAD software.

In my early memory, caculation were always doing with a paper and a pen, and physicists had to verify theories by experiments. With the development of IT technology,we can see experiments could be simulated by computer simulation to verify the theories with a faster speed and a more exactly result. IT technology has otally changed the style of R&D activities.

On the other hand, IT technology have also brought a great revolution for the market and daily life.We could learn faster with the help of search engines, and purchase what we need at onlie-stores. The gradual improvement of IT technology has also helped information spreading and interaction. As I see, broadcast technology has opened up a new channel for entertainment and education.
Children from remote areas could be educated by distance learning. I think this is the greatest contribution for our sociaty.

I think I am both a good team player and a self-manager with a nice ability of self-learning.Also,I am able to work under great pressure.Besides that, I like playing accordin to reduce my pressure.Besides, I have a nice habit to make a regular conclucion that could make me improve faster. As I finnished several develope projects, I see the Internetional technonolgy and data is everywhere to change and help our work. So I’m trying to find an oppotunity to open up new horizons in this area and to make more creations with a nice team.
